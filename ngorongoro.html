Frequently Asked Questions

Planning your trip? Find answers to the most common questions from travelers.

Do I need a visa to visit Tanzania?

Yes, most international visitors require a visa. You can apply online (eVisa) or get it on arrival at major airports.

Is Ngorongoro safe for tourists?

Absolutely. It’s one of the safest destinations in Tanzania, with friendly locals and professional tour operators.

Can I self-drive in Ngorongoro?

Yes, but it's recommended to go with a guide. Some areas require a certified guide for safety and conservation reasons.

What’s the weather like?

Cool mornings and evenings, warm afternoons. It can be misty in the crater, so pack layers and a light jacket.
